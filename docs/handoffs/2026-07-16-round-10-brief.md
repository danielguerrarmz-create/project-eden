## 7. Projects — the divider line must not move

(title, description) below, split by a line.

line **moves**, and that produces a visual discontinuity as you page between projects.

it. It never moves.

and **`object-fit: cover` is banned on heroes.** Fix the divider by fixing the region geometry,
not by cropping heroes to fit. Trap #1 (the wrong-shape box) is precisely how this gets faked.
